Cheeseburger

Description

A delicious cheeseburger made with all american beef, cheese, and bacon.

The best part is the special sauce! It's creamy, spicy, and packs a punch!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of 80/20 Ground Beef
  • 2 slices of american cheese
  • your favorite bun
  • your favorite toppings
  • a bottle of our special sauce for $9

Steps

  1. Craft your beef into patties
  2. cook them until desired readiness
  3. melt cheese on top
  4. slap them on the bun
  5. add toppings and sauce
  6. enjoy!
