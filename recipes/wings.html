Wings

Description

Wings so fresh you'll have to kill the chicken.

Nothing makes spicy wings better than knowing the chicken that died for them.

Ingredients

  • Your childhood favorite chicken
  • 1 stick of butter
  • your favorite hot sauce
  • chives if you like to look fancy

Steps

  1. Slaughter the chicken, defeather it, and bring only the wings back to the kitchen
  2. Deep fry in oil until crispy
  3. melt butter in bowl and mix with your favorite hot sauce
  4. toss your ol' chickens old wings around in the mixture
  5. eat and enjoy!
